Pork Loin Roast

Cook Time: 8 hours

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

Pork roast/loin................ 1-4 lb
Taco seasoning................. 2 Packages
Light beer..................... 1 Can
Medium salsa................... 1 Jar
Dried pinto beans(rinsed)...... 1 bag
Tortillas
Sour Cream
Guacamole
Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients into crockpot.
  2. Cover with enough water to cover the contents.
  3. Cook for 8 hours on low.
  4. Put on tortillas, cheese dip, sour cream, guac and shredded cheese.
