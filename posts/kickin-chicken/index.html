Kickin’ Chicken

Servings: 1

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

High Temp oil               Enough to cover pan
Chicken                     1 Breast (Cut thin)
Ranch                       To taste
Buffalo Sauce               To taste
Flour                       1 Cup
Bread                       2 Slices

Steps

  1. Coat chicken breast in flour
  2. Preheat oven to 400
  3. Put enough oil in pan to mostly cover chicken breast
  4. Slowly place chicken in oil
  • Cook on each side for 3 minutes or until golden and crispy
  1. Place in oven until chicken is fully cooked through.
  2. Toss chicken in buffalo sauce
  3. Toast bread and butter
  4. Place chicken on bread with ranch
