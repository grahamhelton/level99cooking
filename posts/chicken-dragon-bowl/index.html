Chicken Dragon Bowl

servings: 2

cook time: 25 minutes

Ingredients:

Chicken breast...........1 LB
Kewpie mayo..............3 tbsp
Siracha..................1 tbsp
Sushi rice...............2 cups
Garlic chili sauce.......1/4 cup
Soy sauce................1/4 cup 
Brow Sugar...............1/4 cup
Rice vinegar.............1 tbsp
Sesame seeds (optional)

Cookware

  • Rice cooker (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Start cooking sushi rice in rice cooker (2 Cups)

  2. Dice chicken (1LB).

  3. Mix Soy sauce (1/4 Cup), Brown Sugar (1/4 Cup), Garlic chili sauce (1/4 Cup), and rice vinegar (1 tbsp), and diced chicken.

  4. Let sit for a few mintues then cook chicken in skillet until done.

  5. Mix kewpie mayo with rice vinegar, soy sauce,siracha.

  • kewpie mayo: 3 tbsp
  • rice vinegar: 1 tsp
  • siracha: 1 tbsp
  • soy sauce: 1 tbsp
  1. Combine rice, chicken, and top with kewpie mayo mix.
